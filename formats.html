Different Types of Cricket Matches

Test Cricket

Test cricket is the longest format of the game, played over five days with unlimited overs. It tests a team's endurance, strategy, and skill. It‚Äôs known as the purest form of cricket.

One Day Internationals (ODIs)

ODIs are 50-over matches played in one day. Teams wear colored jerseys, and the game balances patience and aggressive play. It's the format used in the ICC Cricket World Cup.

Twenty20 (T20)

T20 is the shortest and most entertaining format, limited to 20 overs per side. It's fast-paced, full of excitement, and popular for leagues like IPL and the T20 World Cup.
