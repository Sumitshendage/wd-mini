What is Cricket?

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game between two teams of 11 players. It's played in many formats and is widely followed in countries like India, Australia, and England.

Game Formats

  • Test: 5-day matches, classic format
  • ODI: 50-over one-day matches
  • T20: 20-over short-format entertainment

Popular Tournaments

  • ICC Cricket World Cup
  • ICC T20 World Cup
  • IPL (Indian Premier League)
  • The Ashes (ENG vs AUS)
  • Asia Cup
